HackRVA November Newsletter
 

November 2, 2022

Wednesday, November 2, 2022 6:30 PM

HackRVA Space Improvement

Once a month, we ask members to come in to work on our shared workspace, and concentrate on this project we all share. We have no staff, but that means WE are the staff. Tasks can include, but are not limited to: cleaning up, working on the website/wiki, identifying and fixing/labeling equipment, developing training processes, and a lot more! Take a look around, and find anything out of place, whether tools or trash, and deal with it.

Cost: $free

November 3, 2022

Thursday, November 3, 2022 7:00 PM

HackRVA Open House!

Come show off a project, socialize, or just hang out. Thinking about becoming a member, come on in, and tour the space. All are welcome! Thursday night has always been our social night when members hang out together. There is no program, agenda, or activity for open house. We just hang out and we invite you to come and sit with us, and get a tour if you like. You may park in our lot. Hack.RVA is a member-run non-profit makerspace located in the Scott’s Addition neighborhood of Richmond, Virginia. We’re essentially a space filled with tools, computers, and people who like to build, invent, tinker, learn new skills and expand their minds. In our space you’ll find people into electronics, robots, wood working, embedded software, metal working, music, art, video, photography, 3D printing, sewing, textiles, virtual reality, gaming, lasers and much more. If you'd like to get some orientation about what HackRVA is and what we do before visiting, please check out our website at hackrva.org (http://www.hackrva.org).

Cost: $free

November 9, 2022

Wednesday, November 9, 2022 7:00 PM

Monthly Electronics Night

Come to Hack.RVA for a night of hardware and electronics on the second Wednesday of the month. We bring our projects to share and problems to discuss with the group. If you're new to electronics, this is a great opportunity to ask questions and learn; let us know what you want to learn more about in the RSVP and we'll help get you some answers.

Cost: $free

November 29, 2022

Tuesday, November 29, 2022 6:30 PM

Open Code: log in and code

This is an online event, and we will send the link to all who RSVP when the time is near. All are welcome, whether just starting out or level 9000! Come on in, jam on some code. Bring your project or work through an online tutorial. We recommend freecodecamp.org Time is 630pm to 830pm -- the last Tuesday of the month. There will be code knowledgeable people around for explanation or conversation. We will try to help each other with what we know, sharing tips and tools. Happy hacking!

Cost: $free
